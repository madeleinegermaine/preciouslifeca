What is Energy Work?

We carry a lot of stuff. Everything we touch, touches us. There is tremendous beauty in this, and there are challenges.

Energy work sorts through the complexity to help you release whatever is ready to go. In that spaciousness, you can come back to yourself. From that place, the whole world can open up in new ways.


Sessions draw heavily on Transformative Energy Work™, a system that engages our sticky places, helps release resistance and encourages us to open again and again to the unknown—who we are becoming, who we are here to be.

A typical session starts with a conversation about what brings you to energy work. We can talk about long standing issues or simply what you are bringing to your day. Then, we create a process that prioritizes your own wisdom and symbols, your inner and outer worlds, your relationships, vaster energies, your deep questions and the parts of you that have the answers.

You don’t have to believe in anything to try this practice. If you like, think of energy work as a way to engage the subconscious to shift limiting beliefs and behaviours, like athletes use visualization to improve their performance. If you have spiritual or religious beliefs, there is space for those here. Whatever your background or belief system, all of you is welcome.

The techniques are infinitely adaptable and easy to use. You will learn how to incorporate some of these tools into your own self-care practices during your session.

Sessions are 60 minutes in length and are offered on a sliding scale. We meet on the phone or online.

These practices are made to be used. Reach out for more information or to see if it is a good fit.
