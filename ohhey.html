Madeleine Germaine // preciouslife.ca

Oh hey! I'm Madeleine.


A sensitive kid who didn’t want to believe in ghosts but was surrounded by them. My twelve-year-old self really took the X-Files to heart… except it turns out the truth was actually in here all along. It was just a bit crowded, and I needed to turn on a few lights.

I spent decades engaging the most beautiful healing modalities I could find and learned how to work with being in a complicated body in a complicated life. Herbal Medicine, Somatic Experiencing, Feldenkrais Method, Rolfing, dance, music, skinny dipping, microdosing, Jesus, Buddhism, trying to be good and trying to be bad, all opened parts of me to myself.

Feeling like life could still be so much better, Transformative Energy Work™ helped me drain the heat out of my deepest wounds and put myself back together. Not like new, but improved. Since then I have thrown myself into figuring out how to make this healing thing tick.

I have accompanied people for most of my life. I was a high school teacher, a sex educator, and a harm reduction worker. I’ve worked with seniors and fed strangers. I’ve changed diapers and pulled people out of restraints. I’ve put herbs on a lot of wounds, not all of them physical. I have very few certificates, a whole lot of experience, and I don’t scare easy.

I love energy work and I take it very seriously. Except it is not that serious. It’s just an elixir of beauty, power, healing and magic in a cauldron of your deepest fears and most secret desires for this one infinitely precious human life. What could be more fun than that? And like the little black dress of personal growth, this kind of energy work goes well with just about anything.
